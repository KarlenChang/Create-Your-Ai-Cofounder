## How to Use This

1. **Start with Problem** — if you can't name the problem, nothing else matters.
2. **Be honest** — "TBD" is fine. It means you have a hypothesis to test.
3. **One page** — if you're writing paragraphs, you're overcomplicating it.
4. **Update monthly** — your model will evolve. This should too.
5. **Test assumptions** — this captures what you believe. Reality checks come next.

## Your First Three Steps (Tonight)

1. **Problem:** What's the #1 problem your business solves? One sentence.
2. **Customer Segments:** Who has this problem? One specific group.
3. **Revenue Streams:** How do you make money? One stream with a price.
